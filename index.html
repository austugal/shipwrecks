About the Data

Historical data compiled from publicly available maritime records including UNESCO Underwater Cultural Heritage, Wrecksite.eu, NOAA shipwreck records, Portuguese Arquivo Nacional Torre do Tombo, Spanish Archivo General de Indias, Lloyd's casualty records, and published academic maritime history sources.

Shipwreck locations marked as Confirmed are documented discoveries. Locations marked as Potential are historically plausible estimates based on trade routes, fleet records, and casualty reports of the period. Treasure estimates are speculative — not verified appraisals.

Compiled by Claude.ai (Anthropic) as an educational tool. Not for commercial salvage or academic citation without independent verification. For verified records consult UNESCO, Wrecksite.eu, or national maritime heritage authorities.
