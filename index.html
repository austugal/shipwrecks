HISTORICAL DATA SOURCES

Data compiled from publicly available maritime records including: UNESCO Underwater Cultural Heritage database, Wrecksite.eu, NOAA Office of National Marine Sanctuaries, Portuguese Arquivo Nacional Torre do Tombo, Spanish Archivo General de Indias, Lloyd's of London casualty records, and published academic maritime history sources.

DATA ACCURACY

Locations marked as Confirmed are based on documented archaeological discoveries. Potential locations are historically plausible estimates. Treasure estimates are speculative — not verified appraisals. Historical notes are in English.

This dataset is an educational and historical exploration tool. Not intended for commercial salvage, academic citation without independent verification, or navigational use. For verified records consult UNESCO, Wrecksite.eu, or national maritime heritage authorities.
